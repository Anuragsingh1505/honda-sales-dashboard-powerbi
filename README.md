# 🚀 Honda Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Honda bike sales, customer behavior, and financial performance across different cities and product segments.

The goal is to derive actionable insights related to sales trends, profitability, customer ratings, and purchasing patterns.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
- Excel / CSV (Dataset)

---

## 📊 Key Features

### 1. KPI Overview
- Total Orders
- Gross Profit
- Net Sales Amount
- Cost Price
- Average Customer Rating

### 2. Sales & Profit Trends
- Monthly Gross Profit trends
- Monthly Net Sales trends
- Cost analysis over time

### 3. Customer & Payment Analysis
- Payment mode distribution (Finance, Credit Card, UPI, Cash)
- State-wise contribution

### 4. Detailed Analysis Page
- City-wise performance
- Product-level insights (Bike Model, Color, Segment)
- Profit, Sales, Cost, Insurance analysis
- Customer rating comparison

### 5. Interactive Features
- Filters for Bike Model and City
- Drill-down capability

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Sales peak observed in December, indicating strong year-end demand
- Express shipping customers show slightly higher purchase value
- Certain cities contribute significantly higher revenue and profit
- Customer ratings remain consistently above 4.0, indicating high satisfaction

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing cities
- Promote top-selling bike models
- Optimize pricing and cost strategy to improve profit margins
- Encourage high-value payment methods (finance/credit)
